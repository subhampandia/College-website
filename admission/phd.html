Admission Procedure

  1. Download the Ph.D. program notification.
  2. Pay the application fee via State Bank Collect.
  3. Register with the application number.
  4. Complete the online application form.
  5. Submit hard copies of documents to the University.
  6. Download viva/viva voce admit card from your account.
