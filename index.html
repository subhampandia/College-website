About Us

Excellence in Education Since 1960

Our Mission

To provide world-class education and foster innovation, critical thinking, and leadership among students.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution of higher learning that transforms lives and contributes to society.

Our Values

Integrity, excellence, innovation, diversity, and commitment to sustainable development and social responsibility.

Our institution has been at the forefront of academic excellence for over six decades. With state-of-the-art facilities, distinguished faculty, and a vibrant campus life, we nurture the next generation of leaders, innovators, and change-makers.

Dr. Rajesh Kumar

Chancellor

"It is with great pride that I welcome you to our esteemed university. Our commitment to academic excellence, research innovation, and holistic development ensures that every student is prepared to excel in their chosen field and contribute meaningfully to society."

Prof. Anjali Sharma

Vice Chancellor

"As Vice Chancellor, I am dedicated to fostering an environment of academic rigor and creative exploration. Our university stands as a beacon of knowledge, where tradition meets innovation, preparing students for the challenges of tomorrow."

Dr. Suresh Patel
